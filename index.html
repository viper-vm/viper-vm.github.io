01. About me

I'm a Machine Learning Engineer at Optimoz, where I build production systems around large language models, retrieval pipelines, and the services that hold them together. I like the full loop — from a research idea to a fast, observable API people actually rely on.

Previously, at Rutgers University (ML & Bioinformatics Lab), I researched Human Activity Recognition and Protein Subcellular Localization using CNNs, LSTMs, and Transformers. I hold a B.Tech in CS from IIT Gandhinagar and an MS in CS from Rutgers.
