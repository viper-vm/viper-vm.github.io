How to play

THE CHALLENGE

  1. Press and hold the circle in the middle and keep perfectly still.
  2. Hold for the full 30 seconds. The ring fills as time passes and a seismograph draws every twitch of your hand.
  3. The page will try to break you — a sudden flash, a fake notification, a spider, a decoy cursor. Don't take the bait.
  4. You flinch if you let go early or move too far from where you started.

THE SCORE

Survive and you get a stillness rating and a tremor magnitude. The emoji strip shows how steady you were in each 3-second window — 🟩 rock-solid, 🟨 wobbly, 🟥 flinched.
