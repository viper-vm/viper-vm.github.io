How to play 🥁

MAKE A BEAT

  1. Tap the big pad to lay down a rhythm — 3 to 12 taps. Each tap drops a pip and a clap.
  2. Hit Send it to turn your beat into a link. Nothing is uploaded — the timing is packed into the URL.

CLAP IT BACK

  1. When you open a beat, tap Hear it once to listen.
  2. Then tap it back on the pad as tightly as you can. Your average timing error (in ms) is your score — lower is better.
  3. Send your own beat back and keep the duel going.
