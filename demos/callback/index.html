How to play 🎶

MAKE A LOOP

  1. A drum beat loops. Tap the 5 pads (a pentatonic scale — everything sounds good together) in time.
  2. Your notes snap to the grid and repeat every loop. Hit Send it to pack the loop into a link.

ADD A LAYER

  1. Open a friend's link and you'll hear their loop playing.
  2. Tap Add my layer and play your part on top. Send it back and keep stacking — up to four layers.

Every layer lives in the URL. No accounts, no uploads.
