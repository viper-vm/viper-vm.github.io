How to play

THE TEST

  1. A shape flashes up somewhere on screen — a box, a ring of dots, a triangle, a blob.
  2. Tap where you think its exact center is. One tap, no do-overs.
  3. You instantly see your dot vs. the true center. It auto-advances — 20 shapes, ~45 seconds.

WHAT IT MEASURES

  • Precision — how tightly your taps cluster. Tight = surgeon, scattered = shaky.
  • Bias — the direction your aim consistently drifts, even when you feel dead-on. This is the "which way your brain leans" number, shown as a % of the shape's size.

THE SCORE

You get an identity (Left-leaner, up-right drifter, dead-center…), a bias %, and a precision score out of 100. Share it and see which way your friends lean.

Tip: rotation and size are randomized so you can't memorize — only your true bias survives 20 rounds.
