How to play ⛳

THE SWING

  1. Flick up (drag or scroll) to putt the viewport down the course toward the cup. A bigger flick = a harder putt.
  2. Each flick counts as a stroke. Overshoot and you'll have to flick back up.
  3. To sink it, the viewport has to reach the cup slowly — arrive too fast and it rattles straight over.
  4. A gentle wind drifts you off line, so you can't just coast — keep correcting.
