How to play

THE TEST

  1. A ball rolls in from the left at a steady speed and disappears behind a wall.
  2. Tap anywhere at the exact moment you think its center reaches the far edge of the wall — the glowing exit line.
  3. A ghost of the ball freezes where it really was, and you see how many milliseconds early or late you were.
  4. 8 rounds. Speed and wall width change; later rounds the ball gently slows, so your brain has to predict the physics.

THE SCORE

Your engine rating comes from how tight your timing is. A consistent early or late lean earns you an identity — trigger-happy vs laid-back — plus how many milliseconds into the future your brain is really seeing.
