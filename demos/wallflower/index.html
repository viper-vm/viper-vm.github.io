🌱 WALLFLOWER

How to play 🌱

THE TWIST

This is an idle game that punishes you for playing it. Your wallflower only grows while nobody is looking.

HOW IT GROWS

  1. Move your cursor away from the plant — or switch tabs, or close the page entirely — and it grows in real time.
  2. Bring your cursor near the plant and it feels watched: it recoils and slowly wilts.
  3. It does most of its growing while you're gone. Come back after a day and it'll have shot up.
  4. A splash of 💧 water gives it a boost — but you only get a few a day.

Everything is saved locally. The absurd part is the stat you'll want to screenshot: grown over days, watched for minutes.
