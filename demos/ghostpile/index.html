THREADED IT 🧵

Anyone who threads your link joins the pile — and mints a harder one.

👻 How to play

Fly. Hold anywhere on screen — or press Space — to rise, and release to fall. Thread the winding cave from start to finish without touching a wall.

Dodge the ghosts. Every player who beat this exact link before you is frozen inside the cave as a solid, named, colored ribbon. Brush a wall or a ribbon and you're dead — but retry is instant.

Become a wall. Survive to the finish and your own flight path is baked into the link. The challenge you send back is now strictly harder than the one you got — because your run is the next player's obstacle.

Grow the pile. Each winner adds one ghost. A pile with nine tangled ribbons is nearly unthreadable — and that's the whole brag. No accounts, no server: the entire pile lives inside the URL.
