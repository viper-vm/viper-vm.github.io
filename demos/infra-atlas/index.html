India Infra Atlas — help

What is this?

A living map of India's infrastructure, technology, energy and defense news. Every story is geocoded and pinned where the project actually is — corridors (expressways, metro lines, rail) are drawn as routes, not just dots. The dataset updates itself daily via an automated pipeline that reads credible sources (PIB, ET, The Hindu, Business Standard and others — the same sources the big infra YouTube channels report from).

Map

Drag to pan, scroll to zoom, right-drag or Ctrl+drag to rotate & tilt. Switch Streets / Satellite / Terrain / Dark basemaps top-left. Rail overlays real railway and metro tracks (OpenRailwayMap). 3D drapes the map over true elevation data — try it on Zojila or the Himalayan projects.

Tools (right edge)

📏 Distance: click points along a path, double-click to finish. ⛶ Area: click a polygon's corners, double-click to close. 📍 Mark: drop your own named pins (saved in your browser, exportable). 🗑 clears measurements. Esc cancels a tool.

News

Click any pin or corridor for full details — investment, capacity, status, benefits, sources, and every related update of the same project (clubbed together). Use the chips up top to filter categories, and the timeline at the bottom to scrub through history or press play to watch the country build itself.

Keyboard

/ search · d measure distance · a measure area · t timeline · Esc close/cancel
