How to play 🎣

THE IDEA

Tabfish rewards you for ignoring it. Cast your lure and go do your real work in other tabs — the lure keeps sinking in real time, even while this tab is in the background or closed.

HOW TO FISH

  1. Tap CAST YOUR LINE. Your lure starts sinking.
  2. Switch tabs and work. Your tab title becomes a live depth ticker — 🎣 610m ⋯ Tabfish.
  3. The deeper it sinks, the rarer and bigger the fish that bite — but deep bites take patience.
  4. When a fish bites, your tab title flips to ❗ FISH ON ❗ and the icon turns red. Come back and tap SET THE HOOK within the window, or it gets away.
  5. Time your strike as the line crosses the green zone to land it. Deeper fish have a smaller zone.

THE DEPTHS

  • Sunlit 0–300m — little common fish
  • Twilight 300–900m — squid & mackerel
  • Midnight 900–2000m — lanternfish, viperfish
  • The Abyss 2000–3800m — anglerfish, gulper eels
  • Hadal Deep 3800m+ — legendary monsters

Leave it overnight and you'll wake up in the abyss. Everything is saved locally — no account, no backend.
