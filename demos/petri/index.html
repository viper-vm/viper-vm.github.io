How to play 🧫

THE GOAL

Everyone gets the same dish today. Light up every gold target — using as few seeds as you can.

HOW A ROUND WORKS

  1. Tap empty cells to plant living seeds (you have a limited supply). You can't plant on walls or on targets.
  2. Press ▶ RUN and watch Conway's Game of Life play out — cells with 2–3 neighbours survive, empty cells with exactly 3 neighbours are born, everything else dies.
  3. A target lights up the moment a living cell touches it. Light them all to win.
  4. You get 3 runs a day — reset and re-plant to win, or to win with even fewer seeds.

One seed next to a target works, but a clever pattern can bloom across several at once. New dish daily at midnight UTC.
