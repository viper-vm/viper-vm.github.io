🍕 DOUGHJO

Press on the ball of dough and swirl in circles to spin it. Centrifugal force stretches it into a pizza — go as wide as you dare, but spin too hard and it tears. Every pizza comes out a wobbly one-of-a-kind shape.

How to play 🍕

SPIN IT OUT

  1. Press on the dough and drag in circles. The faster you swirl, the more it spins.
  2. Spinning flings the dough outward (centrifugal force) — it gets wider and thinner, wobbling like the real thing.
  3. Push too far and a seam tears — game over for that ball. Stop while you're ahead and hit ✋ That's my pizza to lock in your diameter.

Nobody's pizza comes out round. That's the fun — yours might look like a country.
