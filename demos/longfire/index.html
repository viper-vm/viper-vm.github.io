🔥 LONGFIRE

How to play 🔥

KEEP IT ALIVE

  1. Your flame burns fuel in real time — even while this tab is closed or your phone is asleep.
  2. Come back and 🪵 add a log before the fuel runs out. Tap the flame to blow the embers for a small boost.
  3. If the fuel hits zero while you're away, the flame dies — and you'll see exactly when it happened.
  4. The whole point is longevity: how many real days can you keep one flame going?

PASS THE TORCH

Hand your flame to a friend with 🔥 Pass the torch — it makes a link that carries the flame's whole age. They keep it burning from where you left off, like an Olympic relay.
